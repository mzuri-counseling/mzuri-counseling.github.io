Trauma Therapy & PTSD

Trauma is not only defined by major life events, but also by the ways experiences are stored in the body, shaped by relationships, and carried across generations. The practice provides trauma-focused therapy to help individuals process difficult experiences, improve emotional regulation, strengthen relationships, and move toward lasting healing and resilience.

  • Intergenerational Trauma
  • Cultural & Identity-Based Trauma
  • Religious Trauma
  • Sexual Trauma
  • BIPOC & Minority Mental Health
  • Grief & Loss

👁

Brainspotting Therapy

Brainspotting is a powerful brain-body therapy designed to access and process trauma stored deep within the nervous system. Often beneficial for individuals who feel stuck despite insight, traditional talk therapy, or previous treatment.

Brainspotting helps access areas of the brain where unresolved trauma and emotional distress may remain stored. Brainspotting therapy supports deeper emotional processing, nervous system regulation, and lasting healing for trauma, anxiety, emotional overwhelm, and persistent patterns that have been difficult to resolve through other approaches.

✦

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is an evidence-based trauma therapy that helps the brain reprocess distressing experiences that continue to affect emotions, relationships, and daily functioning.

EMDR therapy can help reduce emotional overwhelm, trauma-related triggers, anxiety, and PTSD symptoms while supporting healthier emotional regulation and long-term healing.

🌱

Substance Use & Addiction Counseling

Substance use is often connected to deeper emotional pain, trauma, stress, or difficulties with emotional regulation. The approach focuses on understanding underlying emotional patterns while helping clients build healthier coping strategies, emotional awareness, and sustainable long-term change.

The goal is not only to reduce or eliminate substance use, but to help clients build a more stable, regulated, and fulfilling way of living. Treatment is approached without judgment, with a focus on building emotional awareness, stability, and overall well-being.

Couples Counseling

Relationship challenges often develop through patterns of disconnection, unmet needs, and communication difficulties. Couples therapy at Mzuri Counseling & Wellness focuses on helping partners understand these patterns, improve communication, and rebuild trust, emotional safety, and connection.

The work supports couples in moving from cycles of conflict toward a more secure and fulfilling relationship.

Anxiety & Depression Therapy

Anxiety and depression can impact emotional well-being, relationships, motivation, and daily functioning. Therapy focuses on addressing both the emotional and nervous system factors contributing to distress while helping clients develop healthier coping strategies and more balanced ways of relating to themselves and others.

Cultural, Religious & Identity-Based Trauma

Brain-Body Trauma Therapy

Brainspotting Therapy in New Jersey

Brainspotting is a brain-body therapy used to process trauma, anxiety, and emotional blocks stored deep within the nervous system.

The approach allows access to areas of the brain that may not be reachable through traditional talk therapy, supporting deeper emotional processing and release. By identifying specific eye positions that correspond to areas of stored emotional pain, Brainspotting works directly at the subcortical level — where trauma lives in the body.

Brainspotting therapy at Mzuri Counseling & Wellness is integrated within a broader whole-person approach focused on nervous system stabilization, emotional regulation, and lasting healing.

Ask About Brainspotting →
👁

How It Works

A therapist helps identify a "brainspot" — an eye position that activates a stored trauma response — then supports the client in processing the experience through focused, mindful attention.

🧠

Beyond Talk Therapy

Brainspotting accesses the subcortical brain — the area responsible for emotion, survival, and body sensation — reaching what insight and conversation alone often cannot.

🌿

Who It Helps

Particularly effective for individuals who feel stuck despite previous therapy or insight, and for those whose trauma responses feel physical, wordless, or deeply ingrained.

✦

Integrated Approach

At Mzuri, Brainspotting is used alongside EMDR, CBT, and ACT to provide a comprehensive, whole-person framework for healing trauma at every level.
