Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my insurance benefits?

Contact the member services number on the back of your insurance card and ask about your outpatient mental health benefits, including your copay, deductible, and whether a referral is required.

What payment methods are accepted?

Private pay sessions can be paid via credit card, debit card, HSA, or FSA. Payment is due at the time of service.

Is Brainspotting covered by insurance?

Brainspotting is offered as a private pay service due to the specialized and extended nature of the work. It is not billed through insurance plans at this time.

Are couples sessions covered by insurance?

Couples counseling is offered on a private pay basis only and is not billed through insurance.

What if I don't have insurance?

Please reach out directly to discuss your situation. We're happy to talk through options to help make care as accessible as possible.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes — a brief initial consultation is available to help determine whether Mzuri Counseling & Wellness is the right fit for your needs and goals.

Have Questions About Fees?

Reach out and we'll be happy to help clarify coverage and costs before your first session.
